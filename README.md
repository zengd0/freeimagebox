# 🛡️ FreeImageBox - Privacy-First & Offline Web Utilities

[**👉 Access the Tools: freeimagebox.com**](https://freeimagebox.com)

**FreeImageBox** is a curated collection of high-performance, local-first web utilities. All processing happens entirely within your browser—**no server uploads, no data leaks, and zero privacy risk.**

---

## 🛠️ Core Tools

### 📸 Image & Photography

* [**Smart Image Compression**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/image-compressor/) – High-quality compression for JPG, PNG, WebP, and HEIC without losing visual clarity.
* [**HEIC to JPG Converter**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/heic-to-jpg/) – Batch convert Apple HEIC/HEIF photos to standard JPG locally.
* [**AI Background Removal**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/bg-removal/) – Automatically remove or replace backgrounds using browser-based AI models.
* [**Watermark Removal & Restoration**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/watermark-removal/) – Brush to remove unwanted objects or watermarks and restore image details instantly.

### 📄 Document & Data Utilities

* [**PDF to High-Res Images**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/pdf-to-image/) – Convert PDF pages into high-quality JPG or PNG images with customizable output settings.
* [**JSON Formatter & Validator**](https://freeimagebox.com/tools/json-formatter/) – Securely format, validate, and highlight JSON data without exposing sensitive information to a server.

---

## 🚀 Why Choose FreeImageBox?

* **🔒 100% Private:** Everything runs on your device. Your files and data never touch our servers.
* **⚡ Blazing Fast:** Eliminate upload and download wait times. Results are generated instantly via client-side processing.
* **📶 Offline Ready:** Once the page is loaded, the tools function perfectly even without an internet connection.
* **📱 Simple & Clean:** No registration, no ads, and fully responsive for both desktop and mobile browsers.
